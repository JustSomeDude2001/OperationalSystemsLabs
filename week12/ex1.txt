0000-001f : dma1
dma 1 is direct memory access controller. Most likely used for management of IO devices that use direct memory access as a method of communication with the system.

0020-0021 : pic1

programmable interrupt controller. Helps CPU to handle direct interrupts from either DMA, or other devices that use interrupts to communicate with the system.

0050-0053 : timer1

This is most likely a timer inside the microcontroller unit.
